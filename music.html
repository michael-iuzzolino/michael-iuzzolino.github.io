Music

Guitar, electronica, and everything in between.

I picked up a guitar at 14 and it consumed my life. I studied at Musicians Institute in Hollywood, recorded an album at 18 with my band Structure of Inhumanity, toured across the country, attended Mattias Eklundh's Freak Guitar Camp in Sweden, and recorded music videos. At 22, I quit cold turkey — sold every piece of gear, deleted my YouTube channel, and walked away.

A decade later, the itch came back. I'm playing again on a collection of Caparison, Strandberg, and Fender Strats, and occasionally writing electronica. More on SoundCloud and YouTube.
